About

Arben Lila

Program Manager at Kosovo Advocacy & Development Centre (KADC), PhD candidate, and lecturer at AAB College. His work connects technology, public policy, civil society advocacy, education, sport governance, and institutional development.

Teaching focus Computer Networks, Information Systems Architecture, Android & Modern Mobile Development, Cybersecurity in Healthcare
Research background Telecommunications, SDH/SONET, ATM, IP, MPLS, Resilient Packet Rings, performance modelling
Program work Public-health advocacy, environmental policy, circular economy, ICT reform, donor coordination
Leadership & sport governance Outdoor leadership, mountain sports development, institutional governance, national delegation management, mountain safety and rescue coordination
